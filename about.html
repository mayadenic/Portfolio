About Me

My name is Maya Denic. I am a current second year student at Illinois State University and graduated from Prospect High School. I am a game design major with a minor in music. I know how to play seven instruments (piano, recorder, flute, marimba, piccolo, alto flute, and bass flute). I started to do graphic arts my senior year of high school but I had an interest in it since my freshman year. Due to my busy schedule with being in the Prospect Marching Knights, concert band, and symphonic band, I never had the time to fit graphic arts into my schedule. Thankfully, that passion was still in me and I continue to make great works of art. I mostly do Adobe Photoshop work but sometimes I also do vinyl/printing designs on Adobe Illustrator. I also do drawings on Procreate and photography when I have the chance. I hope to design more in the future! Please take a look at all of my artwork so far on the "Gallery" page! If you ever need help with coding in html or css, any homework or assignments, or anything in between, contact me with either of my emails or through my Discord below!

About Me

All music featured on this website is cut down by me for ice skating purposes

My name is Maya Denic. I am a current third year student at Illinois State University and graduated from Prospect High School. I am a game design major with two minors in music and film studies. I know how to play eight instruments (piano, recorder, flute, marimba, piccolo, alto flute, bass flute, and alto recorder). I started to do graphic arts my senior year of high school but I had an interest in it since my freshman year. Due to my busy schedule with being in the Prospect Marching Knights, concert band, and symphonic band, I never had the time to fit graphic arts into my schedule. Thankfully, that passion was still in me and I continue to make great works of art. I mostly do Adobe Photoshop work but sometimes I also do vinyl/printing designs on Adobe Illustrator or using my Cricut machine. I do drawings on Procreate and photography when I have the chance. I also commentated for the Smash Weeklys tournaments whenever I was not competing held by the ISU Super Smash Bros Club and I commentated for the Varsity Smash Bros team through Redbird Esports. I am currently a media coordinator for Amplify (a club for BRMM members) and I just joined the BRMM marketing team! I hope to design more in the future! Please take a look at all of my artwork so far on the "Gallery" page!
